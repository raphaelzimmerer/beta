LoRaWAN: The Future of Wireless Networking

(Long Range Wide Area Network) is an innovative wireless technology that has been specially developed for the Internet of Things (IoT).

Introduction

Long Range

LoRaWAN enables communication over several kilometres • ideal for applications in rural areas or large industrial plants

Low Energy Consumption

Thanks to the energy-efficient protocol, LoRaWAN devices can be operated for years on a single battery • perfect for IoT applications

Cost Efficiency

LoRaWAN is cost-effective to implement and operate • ideal for large-scale IoT networks with many sensors

Security

LoRaWAN offers end-to-end encryption that protects data transmission securely and against unauthorised access

Scalability

LoRaWAN networks can be easily expanded and customised • from small local solutions to large-scale applications

Versatile Applications

LoRaWAN is used in smart cities, agriculture, Industry 4.0 and environmental monitoring • the possibilities are almost limitless
